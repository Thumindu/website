Here Are Some Facts About Tea...

Black Tea

• Black teas are fully fermented type of tea.This is the most common type of tea from Sri Lanka.Black tea is generally stronger in flavor than other teas.

Green Tea

• Originating from China, green Tea is light in colour, unfermented, maintaining a generally mild and light and sometimes astringent characteristics.

White Tea

• White tea, known in Sri Lanka as Silver Tips, is amongst the most rare and refined of teas for the discerning tea drinker.
