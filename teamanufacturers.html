Here Are Some Popular Tea Brands...

Dilmah Tea

• Dilmah is a Sri Lankan brand of tea, sold internationally. The company was founded in 1988 by Dr.Merrill J Fernando. Dilmah is one of the foremost Tea brand in the world currently serving more than 100 plus countries. Dilmah brought tea that is freshly packed at source and therefore rich in flavour and natural goodness making it one of the finest teas of the world.

For more info click: Dilmah

George Steuarts Tea

•Established in 1835 by brothers James and George Steuart,George Steuart & Company stands proud today as the oldest mercantile establishment in Sri Lanka. In its span of nearly two centuries George Steuart & Company has made immense contributions to establish Sri Lanka as one of world's foremost producers of finest quality teas.

For more info click: George Steuart

Jones Tea
